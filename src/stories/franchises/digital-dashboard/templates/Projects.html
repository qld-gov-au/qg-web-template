DCYJMA Unify Program Stage 2 Project On track $34.4 million 01-07-2021 31-12-2022
  • Objectives:

    Unify is a multi-year program designed to replace the current integrated client information system (ICMS) with a contemporary case and client management system. The Program will have a broad focus on supporting staff, continuing service reforms and enabling more streamlined processes. It will also improve information sharing and collaboration across Queensland Government, social services and justice sectors while engaging with young people, families, carers and services.

  • Expenditure:
    • Cost to date: $34.4 million
    • Approved expenditure: $34.4 million
    • Project commencement allocation: $57.7 million
  • Timeframe:
    • Start date: 01-07-2021
    • Approved end date: 31-12-2022
  • Project journey & reasons for variance:

    • Scope Change: Cost Re-evaluation: Delivery Delay:

    December 2022: Unify Program Stage 2 Project closed on 31 December 2022 having delivered three functional business products, as well as making significant progress on technical work packages, enterprise architecture and whole of program artefacts. Remaining deliverables will form part of the final Program stage commencing in January 2023, to be reported as the Unify Program Completion of Tranche 1. Unify Program Completion of Tranche 1 will adopt a new delivery approach whereby products will be delivered in a single release window, as opposed to progressive product releases, to reduce complexities associated with users having to transition between multiple systems to complete tasks.

    November 2021: Unify Program Stage 2 incorporates the Unify Program Stage 2 Detailed Planning Project, which continued the analysis and business design activities for the products being delivered in Stage 2 and closed 30/11/2021.

  • Priority: Critical
  • Stage: Closed
